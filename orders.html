Nishans of Qamarshan

Royal awards bestowed by the Sheikh Ul Qamarshan to honour service, merit, and humanitarian endeavour.

There are four principal Nishans, listed in descending order of precedence:

Nishan e Imtiaz — Order of Distinction

The highest honour, presented to those considered a blessing to humanity — for exceptional, uplifting contributions.

Nishan e Muhtasham — Order of Magnificence

Conferred for magnificent achievement in service of the Mashaykhat or the greater good.

Nishan e Shafqat — Order of Kindness

Recognises humanitarian work and acts of compassion, especially in relief and welfare.

Nishan e Iftikhar — Order of Glory

Awarded for distinguished accomplishment and impact across diverse fields.
