Geography & Nature

Qamarshan claims 0.277 km² in and around Ramna Park, featuring a lake, landscaped avenues, and dozens of species of flowering, fruit-bearing, and medicinal plants, with multiple gates and walkways.

Currency & Economy

Dīnār (DNR) serves as a gold-standard intermicronational currency; the Coin Bank of Sheikh acts as the state bank.
