White Wolves

Qamarshan historically avoided maintaining a standing armed force. On 24 Muharram 1443 AH (3 September 2021), the White Wolves were announced as an independent protective formation to safeguard the Mashaykhat and aid allies when called upon.

The ethos of the White Wolves emphasizes defence, order, and service over aggression.
