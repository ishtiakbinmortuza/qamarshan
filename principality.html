Principality of Lamdam

Motto: “Beauty lies within”

Lamdam is an autonomous feudal state within the Mashaykhat Qamarshan. It aligns under the absolute monarchy while maintaining its feudal traditions and local stewardship.

Languages include Meitei alongside Bangla and English; the culture reflects its own identity in harmony with the broader polity.
